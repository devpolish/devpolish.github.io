Hello There!

I’m a Senior Backend Developer passionate about Data Science and Deep Learning. I dedicate most of my free time learning about Computer Science and also write articles about it. I have more than 10 years studying programming and mathematics. I love to know how everything works behind scenes. I’m always focused on finding the proper and simplest solution to solve a problem and, supporting my team in order to achieve our goals.

I support Open-Source projects on Github and at companies such as Mercado Libre and openSUSE. I'm the author of several articles in Medium and AppSignal.

Since the beginning of 2018 I started my own company based in Tallinn, Estonia called Bit Concept IO dedicated mostly to provide high-quality and scalable software solutions for worldwide medium and startup companies, likewise, coaching and advising new development teams.

I also work as Chief Technical Advisor at Mgma Sports, where we help our users to maximize their return on their investment in sports with tools that help track their performance, manage connections and teams, and share their most valuable content.
